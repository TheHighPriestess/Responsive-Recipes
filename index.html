ELIZABETH'S RESPONSIVE RECIPES

A personal collection of recipes (gathered from books & websites) put into a very simple responsive website. If the viewport falls below 768px both the introductory text that you're reading now and the recipes will stack.

This website started as a way to learn the basics of responsive web design. I was reading Responsive Web Design by Ethan Marcotte (Recommended) and decided to actively follow along by creating a site for my favorite recipes.

I first started cooking when I was around 12 years old. On the way home from school I used to stop off at the main branch of the Multnomah County Library in order to browse through the cookbooks. I must have been rather ambitious because one of my first culinary creations was an exotic chicken dish involving cherries. By the time I was 15 I was doing most of the cooking at home.

I find it shocking that many people today, especially women, don't know how to cook. I can't imagine not knowing how to cook. Everyone should know how to cook. It really is a necessary skill.
