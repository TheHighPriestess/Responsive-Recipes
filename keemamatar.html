KEEMA MATAR - GROUND BEEF & PEAS CURRY

back to recipe list

This is another one adapted from Mamta's Kitchen. The original recipe is here. I've made some adjustments to it. (If you like Indian food Mamta's Kitchen is a terrific resource for recipes.)

ingredients

500 grams ground beef

200 grams frozen peas

200 grams finely chopped onion (I use red onions)

200 grams finely chopped fresh tomatoes

2 large garlic cloves, grated

0.5 inch fresh ginger, grated

oil

1 teaspoon cumin seeds

1-2 bay leaves

1 inch cinnamon stick

2 large green cardamoms

5-6 cloves

5-6 black peppercorns

0.5 teaspoon turmeric powder

0.5 teaspoon cayenne powder

1.5 teaspoons coriander powder

salt to taste (I usually use around 1 teaspoon)

0.5 teaspoon garam masala powder

instructions

Heat oil in pan and when it's hot add the cumin seeds. When they start to crackle add the onions and fry until they're a nice golden brown.

Add the garlic and ginger to the onions and fry for 30 seconds or so.

Add the ground spices except the garam masala and fry for a few seconds.

Add the chopped tomatoes and fry over low heat until the tomatoes completely dissolve and you have a thick, brown-ish goo...almost like a paste.

Add the ground beef, brown it, and then add the peas and 1/2 cup of water. Cover and simmer for 20-30 minutes.

When it's done add the garam masala, adjust the salt and sprinkle with fresh coriander.
